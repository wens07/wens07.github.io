Divide and Conquer(Main Thoery)

if a>=1 and b > 1, T(n) = aT(n/b) + f(n)

  1. case 1
    $$
    f(n) = O(n^{\log_b{a-\epsilon}}) \longrightarrow T(n) = \Theta(n^{\log_ba})
    $$

  2. case 2
    $$
    f(n) = \Theta(n^{\log_ba}) \longrightarrow T(n) = \Theta(n^{\log_ba}\lg n)
    $$
